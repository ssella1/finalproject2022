My Dog Scout

One of my favorite things is my dog Scout. We bought her from a friend of ours when I was a freshman in High School. We liked the name Scout because of the book "To Kill A Mockingbird".

Scout has a very energetic personality and has been impossible to train. She can barely be walked on a leash. However, she is very loving and sweet so we don't mind.
